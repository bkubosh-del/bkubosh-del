Gluten-Free Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 ½ cups fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk wet ingredients separately.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Fold in blueberries.
  6. Fill muffin tins ¾ full.
  7. Bake 18–22 minutes.

Gluten-Free Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups gluten-free flour blend
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup brown sugar (for filling)
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Activate yeast in warm milk with sugar.
  2. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add yeast mixture, butter, and egg.
  4. Knead gently and let rise 45 minutes.
  5. Roll out dough and spread butter, sugar, and cinnamon.
  6. Roll up and slice.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 20–25 minutes.
