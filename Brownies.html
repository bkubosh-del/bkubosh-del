Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free 1:1 flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¾ cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line 8x8 pan with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Mix melted butter and sugars until smooth.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla.
  5. Stir in dry ingredients.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Spread into pan evenly.
  8. Bake about 30 minutes.
  9. Cool completely before slicing.

Blondies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free 1:1 flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup white chocolate chips or butterscotch chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix melted butter and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Add egg, egg yolk, and vanilla. Stir until combined.
  5. Mix in dry ingredients until just combined.
  6. Fold in white chocolate or butterscotch chips.
  7. Spread evenly into prepared pan.
  8. Bake 22–25 minutes, until edges are golden.
  9. Cool completely before slicing for clean cuts.
